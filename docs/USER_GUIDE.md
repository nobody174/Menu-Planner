# Menu Planner – User Guide

Welcome to **Menu Planner** (menuplanner.no)! This guide helps you get started and make the most of the app.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Go to **menuplanner.no**
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email and a password (min. 8 characters, one uppercase, one number)
4. Check your inbox for a **confirmation email** and click the link
5. Log in!

> First time logging in? You'll be asked to create a **household** — this is your family's workspace where all recipes, menus and pantry live.

---

## Your Household

A **household** is your personal space in the app. Everything — recipes, menus, pantry, categories — belongs to your household.

### Adding Family Members

Go to **Household Settings** → **Members** to invite others:
- **Owner**: Full access (you)
- **Editor**: Can add recipes and regenerate menus
- **Viewer**: Can see everything, change nothing

### Family Profiles (Netflix-style)

Under **Household Settings → Profiles** you can add profiles for family members who share your login (kids, spouse). Each profile has its own name and avatar, so the activity log shows who did what.

---

## Categories

Categories are how your recipes are organised. The app comes with defaults like **Chicken**, **Fish & Seafood**, **Pasta & Noodles** etc.

### Adding a Category

1. Go to **Settings** → scroll to **Manage Categories**
2. Type a name in the input field and click **+**
3. Your new category appears in the list and in the menu generator

### Renaming a Category

In **Manage Categories** → click **Rename** next to any category → type the new name.

### Removing a Category

Click **✕** next to a category. Any recipes in that category move automatically to **Uncategorized** so nothing is lost.

### Merging Categories

Click **Merge into...** to move all recipes from one category into another. Useful if you have duplicates.

---

## Recipes

### Importing Recipe Packs

The fastest way to get recipes is to import a pack:

1. Go to **All Recipes** → **Import Pack**
2. Choose a pack (e.g. Norwegian Classics, Summer Favourites, Salads)
3. Click **Import** — all recipes land in your household instantly

### Adding a Recipe Manually

1. Click **Add Recipe** (or go to **/add-recipe**)
2. Fill in the name, description, difficulty, time, and category
3. Add ingredients (one per line)
4. Add instructions (one step per line)
5. Click **Add to Menu** — the recipe is saved to your household

### Editing a Recipe

Go to **All Recipes** → find the recipe → click it → click **Edit**. You can change any field including category, ingredients and instructions.

### Deleting a Recipe

Open the recipe → click **Delete**. If the recipe was part of the current weekly menu, the menu stays but that slot will show as missing next time you regenerate.

---

## Weekly Menu

The weekly menu is the heart of the app — 6 dinners planned for the week.

### Generating Your First Menu

1. From the **home page**, click **Generate New Menu**
2. A category selector appears — tick the categories you want recipes from
3. Click **Generate** — the app picks 6 recipes and shows your week

### Regenerating the Menu

Already have a menu but want to change it?

- Click **Regenerate** on the home page
- Adjust which categories are selected
- Click **Generate** — a fresh menu is created

> The app tries not to repeat recipes used recently (deduplication logic), so you get variety across weeks.

### Favourites

Star any recipe to mark it as a **Favourite**. When generating a menu you can select the **Favourites** category to pull only from your starred recipes.

---

## Pantry

The pantry is your list of ingredients you **always have at home**. These get highlighted on the shopping list so you don't buy things you already have.

### Managing Your Pantry

Go to **Settings** → **Pantry**:

- **Add item**: type in the box and press Enter or **+**
- **Remove item**: click any item to remove it
- **Reset to defaults**: click **Reset to defaults** to restore the full list of common staples (butter, flour, salt, sugar etc.)

> The app stores pantry items in both Norwegian and English — so adding "lemon" also stores "sitron" automatically, and the pantry shows items in your current language.

---

## Shopping List

After generating a menu, click **Shopping List** to see all ingredients for the week combined into one list, organised by category. Items you have in your pantry are **greyed out** automatically.

---

## Settings

Under **Settings** you'll find:

| Section | What it does |
|---|---|
| Pantry | Manage your pantry |
| Manage Categories | Add, rename, merge or remove categories |
| Activity Log | See who did what in your household |
| PIN | Set a 4-digit PIN for quick profile switching |
| Referral | Share your referral link to invite friends |
| Delete Account | Permanently delete your account and all data |

---

## Language

The app supports **Norwegian** and **English**. Switch language in the bottom bar or via the language button. Recipes, categories and menus all update to your chosen language.

---

## Tips

- **Generate often**: The menu regenerator is smart — use it freely to explore different combinations
- **Use categories wisely**: Having 8-10 well-filled categories gives the best variety in menus
- **Keep your pantry updated**: An accurate pantry makes the shopping list much more useful
- **Import packs first, then add your own**: Packs give you a solid base; add your family's own recipes on top
