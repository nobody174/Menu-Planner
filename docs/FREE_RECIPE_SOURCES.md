# Free Recipe Sources

A curated list of legitimate, free recipe sources for expanding your Menu Planner recipe collection.

**Usage:** You can manually copy recipes from these sources into Menu Planner using the "Legg til oppskrift" (Add Recipe) form.

---

## 🇳🇴 Norwegian Recipe Sources

### Major Norwegian Sites (with Creative Commons or explicit permission)

#### 1. **Mat.no** (Norsk Landbruk og Landbruks­økonomisk institutt)
- **URL:** https://www.mat.no
- **License:** Most recipes allow reuse with attribution
- **Content:** Traditional Norwegian recipes, seasonal dishes
- **Recipe Count:** 500+
- **Permission:** Check individual recipe for license
- **How to Use:** Copy recipe text, add to Menu Planner form

#### 2. **Naturdagsvarer.no**
- **URL:** https://www.naturdagsvarer.no/oppskrifter
- **License:** Creative Commons or explicit permission per recipe
- **Content:** Organic, natural food recipes
- **How to Use:** Browse, copy to form

#### 3. **Tine.no** (Dairy cooperative)
- **URL:** https://www.tine.no/oppskrifter
- **License:** Check usage rights on recipe page
- **Content:** Dairy-focused recipes
- **How to Use:** Copy recipe to form

#### 4. **Ica.no Oppskrifter** (ICA Sweden/Norway)
- **URL:** https://www.ica.se/recept (also ica.no)
- **License:** Attribution required
- **Content:** Scandinavian recipes
- **How to Use:** Copy to form

#### 5. **Dinmat.no** (Expired but archived)
- **Status:** Historical archive
- **Content:** Classic Norwegian recipes
- **Note:** Verify attribution when using

#### 6. **Matprat.no**
- **URL:** https://www.matprat.no
- **License:** User-generated (check individual recipe)
- **Content:** Various Norwegian home recipes
- **How to Use:** Copy to form

#### 7. **NRK Matkultur** (Norwegian Broadcasting)
- **URL:** https://www.nrk.no/mat-og-drikke/
- **License:** NRK content, check for reuse rights
- **Content:** Professional Norwegian recipes
- **How to Use:** Copy recipe content

## 📋 How to Import Recipes

### Manual Import Steps
1. Find a recipe on one of the sources above
2. Open Menu Planner → Settings → "Legg til oppskrift" (Add Recipe)
3. Copy recipe information into the form:
   - **Oppskriftens navn** (Recipe name)
   - **Kort beskrivelse** (Short description)
   - **Vanskelighetsgrad** (Difficulty)
   - **Tidsbruk** (Cooking time)
   - **Kategori** (Category)
   - **Ingredienser** (Ingredients)
   - **Fremgangsmåte** (Instructions)
4. Click save
5. Recipe added to your collection!

## ✅ Attribution & Legal

### When You Import a Recipe, Consider:

1. **Check the license**
   - Most recipes are user-generated
   - Some have Creative Commons licenses
   - Some require attribution
   - Some are proprietary (check site terms)

2. **Attribution**
   - Add recipe source in the description field if required
   - Example: "Based on recipe from [site name]"
   - Most free sources welcome sharing

3. **Modifications**
   - You can modify recipes for your family
   - Adjust ingredients, steps, etc.
   - Menu Planner stores your modified version

4. **No Commercial Use**
   - These sources are for personal/family use
   - Do not use recipes for commercial purposes

## 💡 Tips for Finding Great Recipes

### By Cuisine Type
- **Norwegian:** Mat.no, NRK Matkultur, Naturdagsvarer.no
- **Mediterranean:** AllRecipes, Serious Eats
- **Asian:** Food Network, Genius Kitchen
- **Budget-Friendly:** Budget Bytes, Genius Kitchen
- **Healthy:** Serious Eats, Simply Recipes

### By Dietary Preference
- **Vegetarian:** Most sources have categories
- **Seafood:** Norwegian sources excel here
- **Gluten-Free:** Search each site's category
- **Low-Carb:** Budget Bytes, Serious Eats sections

### By Cooking Time
- **Quick (< 30 min):** Filter by time on most sites
- **Slow Cooker:** Food Network
- **Weekend Projects:** Serious Eats
